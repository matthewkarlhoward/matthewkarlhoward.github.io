about

Hello! My name is Matt, and this is my sandbox on the internet. I am currently a PhD candidate at UCSF working in the Coyote-Maestas and Manglik labs. Prior to UCSF, I worked in the Jackrel lab at WashU.

My current work uses deep mutational scanning and other library-based approaches to understand GPCR function, pharmacology, and evolution.

If you're interested, I can be found the following places: email me, Twitter(X), Bluesky, Github, Linkedin, Google Scholar, Orcid.
