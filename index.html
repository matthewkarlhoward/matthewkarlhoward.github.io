Proton-sensing GPCRs

How do cells detect changes in pH? I used deep mutational scanning of GPR68 to map the complete sequence determinants of proton sensing, combining multiphenotypic screening of both expression and function to identify a conserved polar and charged network that acts as a molecular pH sensor.
